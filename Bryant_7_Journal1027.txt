1. The process of creativity is an organized process in which you first brainstorm, then, you create, and finally, you edit to make things better.

2. Step 1: Brainstorm of think of something you want to do.
	Step 2: Create a rough draft of what you believe to be true.
		Step 3: Realize that you were most likely wrong. Therefore, edit your findings.
			Step 4: Finalize your work and try to improve upon your original design if you can.
